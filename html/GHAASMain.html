Introduction

The Global Hydrological Archive and Analysis System (GHAAS)developed by the Water Systems Analysis Group of the Complex Systems Research Center at University of New Hampshire (Durham, New Hampshire), is a collection of home brew software designed for large scale hydrological modeling. GHAAS is not intended to replace commercial software like ARC/INFO GIS, but rather complement the functionality of the off the shelf software with some unique feature which are essential for hydrological modeling.
